Creation

My work is more than a career — it's how I express my values and experiences in the world. Right now I'm focused on projects that foster resilience, community, and evolution. Each initiative is a reflection of an aspect of my journey, built to serve a need I deeply care about.

CO-EVOLVE

CO-EVOLVE is an initiative for inner and outer evolution. It blends cutting-edge science with spiritual wisdom to guide transformative journeys. The aim is to help individuals and communities grow into their fullest potential.

Viking Sauna

Viking Sauna reconnects people with the elements and each other. Inspired by Nordic traditions, this project uses the extremes of heat and cold to bring individuals together in nature. It's a space for community, resilience, and the simple power of being present together.

Resilient Systems (Food & Energy)

Another focus is building community resilience through innovations in food and energy. This involves experimenting with sustainable agriculture and off-grid energy solutions on a local scale. Each project is about meeting basic human needs in an earth-friendly, self-sufficient way — strengthening our independence from fragile systems.
