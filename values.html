Principles of Power

  • Presence — The moment is my compass.
  • Freedom — Real power comes from within, not control.
  • Transparency — No masks, only open honesty.
  • Wisdom — Turning life’s lessons into a guiding light.
  • Love — The core energy behind all transformation.
  • Respect — Seeing the divine in another without losing yourself
  • Responsibility — I honor the power I carry. I take full ownership of my emotions, choices, and their consequences, knowing that what I create within becomes what I create in the world.

"These are not ideals. They are lived experiences, earned through fire."
