Presence

Engineer · Entrepreneur · Former Special Forces · Father · Seeker

I have lived a full life, and on the way I have learnt what it takes to build something real.

Nanotechnology and quantum mechanics showed me the hidden fabric of reality. Entrepreneurship taught me the grind of turning ideas into companies. The special forces forged resilience when everything is on the line. Shamans, yogis, scientists, and coaches cracked me open to transformation of spirit and flesh. Fathering two children, one on earth and one in heaven, connects me to creation power beyond myself.

All of it connects. All of it was preparation.

Now I use every piece — the science and knowledge, the grit and discipline, the continuous personal growth, the love and wisdom from my children — to build foundations for a new world.

Advanced technology. Food systems. Energy production and infrastructure. Communities. Radical new ways of connecting and collaborating. Spaces where people thrive. A future where privacy is sacred, where people are not controlled, but protected by systems that empower them. New ways of governing where freedom and safety go hand in hand, where humans can truly grow, transform, and live with dignity.

I have a global community around me. Family, friends and partners across continents are weaving together companies, farms, retreats, trade routes into resilient infrastructures. These are not dreams. These are realities.

Where are we headed? Towards an advanced human civilisation — conscious, regenerative, resilient, and alive.

If this resonates with you — let’s connect and build.
