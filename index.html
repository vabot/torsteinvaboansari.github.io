My Story

I grew up in a calm but closed religious community in southern Norway called Menigheten Samfundet. My parents were among the more open-minded ones there, which gave me room to explore beyond the boundaries of belief.

Eventually, I broke free from the mental cage of dogma through the study of nanotechnology and quantum mechanics. Here I discovered the deeper fabrics of reality, where reality as we understand it actually break down. Later, meditation and philosophy opened another dimension: the inner universe. That combination naturally led me into entrepreneurship, - the art of turning ideas into something real and valuable for the Earth.

Through entrepreneurship, a seed of divine willpower took root. I became obsessed with exploring the limits of the mind, body, and heart, which guided me into the Norwegian Special Forces, where these limits were tested to the edge.

From the stillness of meditation to the fire of the Arctic, from the discipline of the military to the vulnerability of losing a child. Life has carved me open. I’ve met birth and death, love and loss, power and surrender.

Now, everything I do begins from that intersection, where determination, knowledge, and wisdom meet.
Body. Mind. Spirit.
