Torstein Vabo Ansari

Nanotechnologist · Entrepreneur · Former Special Forces · Father · Seeker

I am Torstein Vabo Ansari — father, husband, explorer, and builder. I have built this website so anyone can find out more about who I am. I want to be honest and transparent. I am just a human, like you, constantly discovering new things about this extraordinary experience of life.

My life has moved between extremes: From the rigidity of a fundamental religious upbringing to breaking the shackles of mind-shrinking dogma in my studies of nanotechnology and quantum mechanics. From the stillness of meditation and the fire of the Arctic, to the absolute power, discipine and willpower of the Norwegian Special Forces. I’ve known birth and death, love and loss, presence and power. Everything I do now begins from the multidimensional intersection of determination, knowledge and wisdom — Body, Mind and Spirit.
